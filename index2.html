04

About

~/about.md

## who

I build the apps people use and the systems behind them — React Native and Flutter on the front, Python and REST APIs on the back. I like owning a feature end to end, from the screen someone taps to the database row it writes.

## how

Software should feel obvious to use and stay boring to maintain. I'd rather ship something small and solid than something large and fragile, and I read my own code again a week later to make sure it still makes sense.

## why

Because building is the fun part — sketching an idea, watching it run, and fixing the thing that breaks at 1am. That part hasn't gotten old yet.
